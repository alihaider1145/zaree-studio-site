Privacy Policy

What we collect How we use it Who sees it Your rights Tracking Contact

01

What we collect

When you fill out the project inquiry form on the Start page, we receive the information you type: your name, email address, which niche you're in, a description of your project, and your budget range.

That's it. We don't collect anything passively — no cookies, no tracking pixels, no analytics scripts running in the background. If you browse this site without filling out the form, we collect nothing.

02

How we use it

Your form submission is used for two things only: replying to your inquiry, and scoping whether your project is a fit for Zaree Studio.

We do not add you to a mailing list. We do not send you marketing emails. We do not sell your information. We do not use it to target you with ads anywhere.

If we don't move forward together, your information isn't kept on file for any other purpose.

03

Who sees it

The only person who reads your submission is Ali — the one person behind Zaree Studio. Your inquiry goes directly to a private inbox. It is not shared with third parties, contractors, or partners.

Form submissions are processed through Formspree, a third-party form handling service. Formspree stores submissions on their servers temporarily to deliver them. You can read their privacy policy at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

04

Your rights

You can ask to see what information we have about you, correct anything that's wrong, or ask us to delete it. Just email us and we'll handle it within 48 hours.

There's no complicated process. If you submitted the form and want the data gone, ask and it's gone.

05

Tracking & analytics

Zaree Studio does not use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or any other analytics or tracking service. There are no third-party scripts running on this site except the Cloudflare email obfuscation script (which protects the email address from spam bots) and Formspree (for the form).

No ads. No retargeting. No data sold.

06

Questions

Privacy questions, correction requests, deletion requests — email us directly. The address is in the footer of every page.
