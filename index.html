Here is exactly what we do, what we don't do, and how the whole system works.

To keep your monthly costs flat and cover the upfront design, hosting, and domain setup, there is a minimum 12-month agreement. After that, it rolls month-to-month. We invest a lot of time into building you a premium system, so we look for partners who are serious about building a long-term, independent brand.

Usually within minutes, but 24 hours maximum. If you need to change a price, swap a food photo, or add a weekend special, just drop us a text. We get it sorted in the background so you can stay focused on the kitchen.

We don't employ drivers, but we have a much better solution. Your website hooks directly into Stuart Logistics. They handle the delivery for a flat distance fee that the customer pays at checkout, not you. You get the delivery order, Stuart handles the rider, and you pay £0 in commission.

By default, every single order drops instantly into your kitchen's WhatsApp. It's clean, simple, and you don't need any new gear. If you want a dedicated tablet application or a direct link into your current EPOS till setup, we can absolutely set that up for you as an add-on feature.

No hidden percentages or tech cuts here. You pay your flat £150 a month via a standard bank transfer to our UK business account (handled securely through WorldFirst). Whether you do £1,000 or £20,000 in takeaway sales this month, you keep every single penny of it.

You text or call us directly. Because you are working with a solo specialist—not a massive corporate ticketing system—you don't get stuck in an automated support queue. Your order flow is your livelihood, so we treat tech emergencies with the exact same urgency you do.
