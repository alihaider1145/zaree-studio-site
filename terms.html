Who These Terms Are For

These terms apply when an independent restaurant, cafe, takeaway, or home kitchen (that's you) works with Zaree Studios (that's us) to build, host, or manage a direct ordering website.

02

What We Build

We design and build a custom ordering website for your kitchen, set up direct order routing to your WhatsApp, and connect delivery through Stuart Logistics where you'd like it. Depending on the package you choose, we also handle ongoing updates, hosting, and support.

03

Packages & Payment

Our current packages, pricing, and what's included in each are listed on our Pricing page and may be updated from time to time. Whatever's agreed at the point you sign up is what applies to your project.

One-time packages are typically paid in two instalments — part upfront, part on completion. Monthly packages are billed flat, with no commission or percentage cuts, however much you sell.

04

Contract Length & Cancelling

Monthly packages run on a minimum 12-month term to cover the upfront design, hosting, and setup work — after that, it rolls month-to-month and you can leave whenever you like. This keeps your monthly cost flat instead of us needing to charge a big upfront fee.

Need to cancel before the minimum term is up? Message us directly and we'll talk it through — we'd always rather have an honest conversation than hide behind a penalty clause.

05

What We Need From You

To build a great site quickly, we'll need your menu, food photos, branding (if you have any), and reasonably prompt replies while we're building. The faster you get us what we ask for, the faster your site goes live.

06

Ownership & Your Website

Once your website is paid in full, it's yours — your menu, your branding, your content. For founding clients who joined under a portfolio or testimonial arrangement, we may agree separately to showcase the work in our own portfolio and marketing.

07
