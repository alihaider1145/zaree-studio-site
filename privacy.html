What We Collect

Form submission data only: name, email, niche, project description, and budget range. No cookies. No tracking pixels. No analytics scripts. If you browse this site without filling in the form, we collect absolutely nothing.

Solely to reply to your inquiry and assess project fit. We will never add you to a mailing list, send you marketing emails, sell your data, or target you with ads. If we don't move forward with a project, your data isn't kept.

You can ask to see, correct, or delete any data we hold about you at any time. Email us directly. Requests are handled within 48 hours.

No Google Analytics. No Meta Pixel. No tracking of any kind. The only third-party services active on this site are Cloudflare (for email obfuscation) and Formspree (for the contact form). No ads. No retargeting. No data sold — ever.
